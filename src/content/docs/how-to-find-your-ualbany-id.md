To find your UAlbany ID#, usually used for purchasing parking permits, just follow these steps:
    1. Go to the MyUAlbany portal and log in.
    2. In the Parking Information section, click the &quot;My Parking Information (On-line Appeals)&quot; link (see attached picture).
    3. At the top of the page there should be an &quot;Empl ID&quot; label with your UAlbany ID# next to it.
